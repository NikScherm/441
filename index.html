Reflection

Over time, many elderly individuals experience a decline in brain activity and memory. Games can help these individuals exercise their brains by engaging in game-play and remembering how to play. Continuing to learn as you age is very important for maintaining mental sharpness and cognitive function, which can worsen with age. We should encourage the elderly to play games. However, I don’t believe the games should be first-person shooters or competitive games like League of Legends, but rather relaxing options such as Animal Crossing or mobile apps like Candy Crush
